## Design Context

### Users
This site is for people evaluating the creator through their ideas and output first: peers, followers, collaborators, early customers, and internet strangers discovering the work from social posts or open-source projects. Their main job is to understand who this person is, what they build, and where to go next.

### Brand Personality
Friendly, energetic, and curious. The site should feel like a thoughtful indie builder with a public body of work, not a polished corporate portfolio. It should create optimism, clarity, and momentum.

### Aesthetic Direction
Playful Geometric in light mode only. The visual system should feel tactile, bold, and content-led, combining clean readable layout blocks with lively Memphis-inspired decoration: hard shadows, primitive shapes, pattern fills, and asymmetric radii. The reference tone is a more expressive version of `leolabs.me`, with higher personality and stronger visual rhythm.

### Design Principles
1. Stable grid, wild decoration: keep content legible and structured while letting the surrounding shapes carry personality.
2. Make bilingual content feel intentional: Chinese leads, English supports, and both must remain readable without clutter.
3. Treat cards like stickers: crisp borders, chunky shadows, and slight physicality on hover.
4. Favor memorable moments over generic polish: bold section transitions, marquee energy, and a distinctive hero silhouette.
5. Respect accessibility: strong contrast, obvious focus states, touch-safe controls, and reduced-motion support.
