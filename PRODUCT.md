# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

This personal homepage is for people evaluating the creator through ideas,
projects, writing, and public work: peers, collaborators, potential customers,
followers, and visitors arriving from social links or open-source repositories.
They need to understand who Sixseven is, what he builds, how he thinks, and
where to go next.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Sixseven as a thoughtful AI Native builder with a clear body
of work. Success means visitors quickly understand the creator's trajectory,
trust the seriousness of the work, and find the right next action: read, inspect
projects, follow updates, or make contact.

## Brand Personality

Professional, restrained, and intellectually curious. The tone should feel
focused, composed, and credible while preserving enough personal texture to make
the page memorable.

## Anti-references

Avoid the polished corporate portfolio pattern: overly formal resume language,
generic case-study tiles, glossy executive imagery, template-heavy sections,
and visual systems that feel detached from the creator's actual work.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with substance: projects, writing, and experiments carry the brand.
2. Keep the interface composed: structure, spacing, and typography should create
   confidence before decoration creates personality.
3. Make bilingual content feel intentional: Chinese leads, English supports,
   and both stay readable at a glance.
4. Show the trajectory: the page should connect education, poker, Web3, and AI
   studio work into one coherent path.
5. Use memorable restraint: signature interactions can add identity when they
   clarify the story.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target strong contrast, clear focus states, keyboard-accessible navigation,
touch-safe controls, readable bilingual typography, and reduced-motion support
for animated sequences.
